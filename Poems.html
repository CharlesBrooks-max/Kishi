Poetry,

Words can never truly measure how much I love you, but I hope these poems let you feel even a small piece of my heart.


Some feelings are too deep for words alone. I may never fully express how much I love you, but I hope these poems paint a picture of the love I carry in my heart every single day.

No matter how many words I write, they'll never be enough to tell you how much I love you. So instead, I hope these poems become the voice of my heart.

Stars are known to be the light within the dark, and it is also the guide for those who are lost.

When those stars are about to die, they shine brighter than those around it illuminating light that gives hope.


perhaps that is what love is,

Not because it is always easy, nor because it never fades, but because even in its quietest moments, it still finds a way to glow.


If words alone is not enough to tell you how much I love you,

let the amount of stars tell you how much I love you.

There is a language softer than the wind,

one that asks for no voice,

and needs no sound.


It is the quiet meeting of our skin,

where time slows down,

and two souls embraced.


Your touch is not merely skin to skin, it is the warmth finding it's way through every insecurity I've hidden.

It is the calm after the storm, it is what gives assurance without lust.

It is where the restless is learns to breathe.


If someday my words fail, let me hands do what my lips cannot say.

For every interaction we've had, be it caressing or hugs we shared, I have already whispered "I love you" a thousand times.

"For those who dare to hope."

Love is not merely a wish upon the stars,

it is the courage of those who believe.


Hope is where choosing you and persisting

throughout every sunrise and moonlit night,

trusting that tomorrow will still hold your hand

as gently as it can be today.


For those who dare,

love becomes more than a feeling,

it becomes a vow between two souls.


and today, as another month passby,

with your name carved within my soul,

I'll forever make that vow to you.

Never judge a book for its cover,

for it can contain a story of the two lovers who were parted by death,

or a story soon to be written.


With every fold that contains the unknown,

only the author can clear the mysteries within.

With our lifespan as the ink that writes the story,

and our decisions to choose where the story goes.


With you and I as the main characters,

I would want the title to be "Souls Intertwined".

May every chapter be filled with laughter that echoes through pages,

ever conflict that will become a stepping stone for growth.


If I were given the chance,

I'd write your name on every page.

not to keep you chained to the story,

but to remind the world that every page is worth reading,

that every chapter that begins with you and ends with you.
