Music,

the one that I personally chose that reminds me of you.


Some words and actions may sometimes not be enough,

but music is the one that fills that gap, so here I present to you the music that reminds me of you.

I chose this music because of the following lyrics that struck me and was reminded of you,

it is the "Wrecking balls and million stones Will never take away My strongest will to say The words you long to hear",

because even if something does happened, or about to, it won't stop me from loving you.


It also sets the mood for me and makes me fall even deeper whenever I think of you while listening to this song.

"Will never take away my strongest will to say" is such a magneficient word that only you comes to my mind.



This song reminds me of how much I fell for your giggles and your unbreakable will.

Especially the part where "she's never been the kind to be hollowed by the stares

on parade frolic and fuck about to make her gaze

Turn to a scribble on a page by a picture that holds her absence

But you're daft to think she'd care"

Reminds me of you so much, and this song also expresses this part "you would rather be beside than in front of"


I would rather be beside you than face you alongside those side characters,

I am your kakampi, partner, best friend, and lover.



This song reminds me of you so God damn much, it is literally the song where we both clicked,

I wasn't aware you were into this type of music, and it literally made my day when I found out.

This song will always have a special place in my heart, it is already self explanatory but I will explain either way.


This song reminds me of your aura so much, the way I can lean on to you,

focus on myself and have time for myself but you will always be there to support me.



You are the reason why my world is filled with colour and songs,

I love you so much baby.
