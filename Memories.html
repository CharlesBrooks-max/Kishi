Our Memories

My beloved Kishi, it really has been awhile ever since we first met.


At first, I never imagined that we would end up together. But here we are, and I couldn't be more happy.

So here I gladly present to you a collection of our memories together, from the first time we met to the present day. I hope that these memories will bring a smile to your face and remind you of all the wonderful moments we've shared.

Baby, I am really glad I met you.

I couldn't or rather, I could never imagine myself without you.

I love you so much, my dearest Kishi.
